Meet Our Team

Nadia, a vital member of our team at Kids Planet Day Care. Originally from Ukraine, Nadia brings a rich cultural perspective to our diverse community. Fluent in both English and Russian, Nadia warmly welcomes and encourages children from all backgrounds and nationalities to thrive in our environment.

With five children of her own, Nadia understands the intricacies of childcare from firsthand experience. She has spent a decade honing her skills as a provider here in America, cultivating a deep understanding of the unique needs and development of young minds in this setting.

Nadia's presence enriches our center, providing a nurturing and inclusive space where children feel valued and supported. Her dedication to each child's well-being and growth makes her an indispensable part of the Kids Planet family.

Vlad, another integral member of our team at Kids Planet Day Care. As Nadia's husband, Vlad shares her passion for providing exceptional care to our children.

With a background that complements Nadia's experience, Vlad brings his own unique perspective to our center. Fluent in multiple languages, including English and Russian, Vlad contributes to our multicultural environment, ensuring that all children feel welcome and included.

Vlad's hands-on approach and genuine care for each child further enhance the nurturing atmosphere of our center. His dedication to supporting children's growth and well-being makes him a valued lead teacher at Kids Planet.

Together, Nadia and Vlad form a dynamic team, enriching the experiences of the children in our care and making Kids Planet a warm and supportive environment for all.

Ilona, our wonderful music teacher at Kids Planet Day Care, not only inspires children to engage in musical activities through singing and dancing but also introduces them to great composers and their works.

She shares her knowledge and passion for music, telling children about the lives and works of classical composers. Her captivating stories about musical geniuses such as Bach, Mozart, and Beethoven capture the children's attention and inspire them to explore new creative expressions.

Thanks to Ilona, our young musicians not only immerse themselves in the world of music but also learn about its great creators, expanding their horizons and deepening their appreciation for the art of music.
