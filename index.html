Where Little Hearts Grow Big

About Us

Welcome to Kids Planet Day Care, where the world of learning and fun comes together! At Kids Planet, we believe that early childhood is the perfect time to embrace the joy of learning, and our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where your child can thrive.

Our Mission

We are offering high-quality child care and education in a warm, supportive atmosphere. Our unique focus on bilingual education ensures that your child will not only be cared for with love and attention but also immersed in the beautiful Russian language and culture. We are passionate about helping children become bilingual, giving them the gift of a second language that will open doors to new experiences and opportunities throughout their lives.

Our Facility

Kids Planet Day Care welcomes children from ages 1 year to 5 years. Our comprehensive program is designed to promote cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development, ensuring a well-rounded early childhood education. Each day, your child will engage in a variety of activities that stimulate curiosity and foster a love for learning.

Why Choose Kids Planet Day Care?

Bilingual Immersion

At Kids Planet, Russian is not just a subject; it is a way of life. From songs and stories to games and everyday conversation, our skilled and caring teachers integrate the Russian language into all activities, creating a natural and engaging learning environment.

Qualified Staff

Our team of experienced educators is dedicated to the growth and development of each child. All our staff members are fluent in both Russian and English, ensuring effective communication and a rich educational experience.

Safe & Stimulating Environment

Our state-of-the-art child care facility is designed to provide a safe, welcoming, and stimulating environment. With age-appropriate activities and materials, children are encouraged to explore, discover, and learn at their own pace.

Parent Partnership

We believe that parents are our partners in the education and development of their children. We maintain open lines of communication and offer regular updates on your child's progress and experiences at our day care.
